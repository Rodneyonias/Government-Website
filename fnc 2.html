Alaminos Longganisa

Don't forget to bring home this most requested pasalubong. Taste the goodness of ground pork and garlic.

Alaminos longganisa is a type of longganisa from the town of Alaminos in Pangasinan. It is made from ground pork, garlic, and local spices like black pepper and bay leaves. It is characterized by its slightly sweet and garlicky flavor.
