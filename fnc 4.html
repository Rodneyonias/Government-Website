Binungey

Try this sticky rice cake cooked inside the bamboo tubes.

Binungey or binongey is a rice cake delicacy in. Pangasinan which is malagkit (sticky rice) soaked in. coconut extract and steamed in bamboo over fire. Rice is soaked in coconut extract then placed inside a bamboo to be steamed over fire. A rich sweet sticky coconut jam is served with it, usually alongside slices of mango.
